Survey Methodology

Diagram and Rational

1. To determine the subject of investigation and design survey plan as the preparation work before the investigation, it is necessary to clarify the purpose, significance and requirements of the investigation and research of the subject, determine the types, methods and methods of the investigation and research, and formulate the investigation plan and investigation outline, etc.

2. To collect data, we need to collect relevant data according to the requirements of the survey design, pay attention to the quality of data collection, find problems in time, and supplement the investigation on the spot.

3. Sorting and analyzing data requires the review, collation and statistics of the data, distinguishing the true from the false, and eliminating the errors in the data to ensure the authenticity, standard, accuracy and completeness of the data.

4. Write research report should focus on the investigation results or research conclusions, and systematically describe and explain the research process, methods and some important issues in the research.
